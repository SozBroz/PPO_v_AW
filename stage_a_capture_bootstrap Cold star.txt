Stage Progression Logic
The curriculum advances based on competence metrics (capture sense, terrain usage, win rate, etc.) with minimum games required in each stage:

Stage A: 200+ games, fast first capture (≤15 steps) + sustained capture volume (≥4)
Stage B: 300+ games, terrain ≥0.52, capture sense ≥0.5, win rate ≥0.45
Stage C: 500+ games, win rate ≥0.62, terrain ≥0.52, episode quality ≥0.72
Stage D: 300+ games, win rate ≥0.58, terrain ≥0.48, capture sense ≥0.42
Stage E: 400+ games, win rate ≥0.52, terrain ≥0.42, episode quality ≥0.68
Stage F: 400+ games, same metrics as E
Stage G: Terminal stage (requires 1M+ games, never advances automatically)
